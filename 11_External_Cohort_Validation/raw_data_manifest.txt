Raw external validation inputs are public GEO/NCBI resources and are intentionally not redistributed in this package.

GEO cohorts:
- GSE122897: raw count table GSE122897_readCounts_raw.txt.gz and series matrix metadata from GEO.
- GSE13353: GEO series matrix, GPL570 annotation.
- GSE15629: GEO series matrix, GPL6244 annotation.
- GSE75436: GEO series matrix, GPL570 annotation.

Gene annotation resources used by the script:
- NCBI gene2ensembl.gz
- NCBI Homo_sapiens.gene_info.gz

The analysis script external_cohort_validation_analysis.R documents the exact processing workflow. Processed signature scores, statistics, gene coverage, key-gene z-scores, and publication-ready figures are included here.
